Study of World Bank’s WDI indicator Life expectancy at birth

This mini-study project was an exercise to learn how to use the World Bank’s API for the World Development Indicator (WDI) repository.

In this study, I explore the indicator Life expectancy at birth and I also do some comparative analisys using other indicators that I expect to be highly correlated with life expectancy: namely GDP and the status of Fragile and conflict affected situations (FCS).

In order to flag the individual countries that are classified as FCS, I used the World Bank list of economies (as of June 2017) found here
