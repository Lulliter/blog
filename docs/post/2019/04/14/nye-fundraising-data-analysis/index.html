NYE fundraising data analysis

Luisa M. Mimmi

2019/04/14

Since March 2018, I have worked part-time as a Pro-Bono Fundraising Data Analyst for the nonprofit organization New York Encounter. I performed several ETL tasks –via SQL-based data transformations and R programming– to set up the integration of data that existed in separate and disconnected platforms. Namely, the Nonprofit had informations on donors and attendees on SendGrid, donations recorded via Square and miscellaneous other information in Salesforce. After cleaning and merging such information, I performed an initial explorative characterization of the Nonprofit private donors. Most of the data integration and analisys was performed in R.
